Weight Lifting

The Lads' Gym has a large free weights area for anyone from intermediate trainers upwards. With our expert instructors on hand to guide you, free weights can change your body shape for the better, faster.

Cycling

Get ready to burn calories and boost your fitness level in our moderate to high intensity classes. Motivating music will provide the soundtrack as you cycle your way through multiple terrains designed to improve cardio endurance, lose weight and strengthen your legs and core.

Yoga

Breathe away stress and find your inner strength with yoga classes and restorative workouts. Come awaken your body and mind – our classes are open to all levels.

Swimming Pool

Whether you’re a serious swimmer or absolute beginner, you’ll find a swimming pool just for you at The Lads' Gym. You’ve got 50 meter swimming pools, 25 metre swimming pools, and heated salt-water pools.

Treadmills

Come try out our extensive range of threadmills. Whether you're looking for a warm up, or to get your miles in, our treamills space will have what you require.

Boxing

As Specialists in this field we run courses to train and support fitness professionals and sports coaches to adapt the age-old art of boxing to deliver a safe, fun and inclusive form of exercise and padwork coaching.
